## 👤 About Me

I'm a robotics researcher specializing in Extended Reality (XR) and medical applications. My M.S. research at DGIST focused on XR-based surgical navigation systems — combining real-time 3D visualization, DICOM medical imaging, and HMD integration to support intraoperative guidance. I am now working at DIGITRACK Inc., developing robotic digital-twin simulations that bridge high-fidelity virtual environments and real-world industrial deployments.
